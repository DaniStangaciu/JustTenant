JusTenant is a website which provides you quick and efficient assistance if you are facing possession proceedings from your Local Authority landlord. We will take you through a six-step process.

  1. Check financially eligibility for legal aid by the GOV website here: www.gov.uk/check-legal-aid.
  2. Check the map for local solicitors and contact one one the solicitors to discuss your case if you are eligible for legal aid.
  3. Fill in the provided form if you cannot find a solicitor to take your case or if you are ineligible for legal aid.
  4. Take the generated report to the Solicitor if you are eligible for legal aid once you have contacted them.
  5. If not eligible for legal aid, take the report to the court and check for the duty solicitor. If the duty solicitor can represent you, you show them the generated report.
  6. If there are no duty solicitors, then you can represent yourself in court by presenting your generated report to the Judge.
