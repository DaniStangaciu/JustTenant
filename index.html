Justenant is a website which provides you quick and efficient assistance for someone who has a housing dispute i.e. repossession.

We will take you through a three step process which will generate a report to summarise your case either to represent yourself at court or with a solicitor.

  1. Check if your a financially eligible for legal aid
  2. If you are then you can check for local solicitors who provide legal aid for housing
  3. Report will be created which you will fill in in order to provide facts about your issue
However if you are not eligible for legal aid, you do not check for local solicitors, you immediately go on to step three - report and fill that in. So, that you can represent yourself in court with that piece of paper summarising your case.
