JusTenant is a website which provides you quick and efficient assistance if you are facing possession proceedings from your Local Authority landlord.

We will take you through a three-step process.

  1. Check financially eligibility for legal aid by the GOV website. If you are not financially eligible then you represent yourself at court with the report.
  2. Check map for local solicitors who offer legal aid for housing.
  3. Generate a report containing the important information about your case. You can show this to your solicitor before you enter court or use for yourself.
